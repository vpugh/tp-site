Role: Designer, Developer
Type of Work: Email Design, Email Development
Client: CDEWorld

CDEWorld is one of the fastest growing online continuing dental education sites and a global leader in dental e-knowledge transfer. Their mission: to be the site of choice for oral healthcare professionals worldwide to source and complete their continuing professional development needs.

Unified Emails For Deeper Student Communication

CDEWorld had new partner come on board for their partner portals. I was tasked with creating emails that would advertise Tufts University School of Dental Medicine upcoming events. The main task would be to replicate the design and feel of Tufts while making it an easy email to develop in a short time.

Approach and Execution

The main objective was keeping the branding unified. I incorporated the already strong branding from the Tufts University School of Dental Medicine website into the emails. I utilized an existing modular email system and extended the Tufts branding but maintained a core email structure. It made replication faster and easier to do on short notice.
