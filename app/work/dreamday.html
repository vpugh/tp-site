Updated Look for a Special Event Company

Giving a Company a Brand Refresh

Role: Designer
Type of Work: Branding
Client: Dream Day Visionaries

Dream Day Visionaries a special event company who excel in combining originality and class to provide a premium experience for their customers.

Approach and Execution

They were looking to refesh their branding, encompassing their branding and branching out to other aspect at a later point. They wanted Tiger lilies to be the inspiration behind their identity, so I took the color and logo from them. The direction I was give was that they wanted Tiger Lilies to play a role in the brand of their company.
