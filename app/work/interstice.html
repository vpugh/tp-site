Simple Minimalist and Clean Theme

Tumblr theme

Role: Design, Development
Type of Work: Web Design, Web Development
Client: Self-initiated

I was inspired to make this theme from seeing the background technique on another site. I wanted to figure out how the half image/half scroll worked, so what better way then to make a theme with it. I kept the design minimalist, with a mix of serif and san-serif for a change of pace. The theme would be full customisable, you can change the image, the colors, fonts and the icon.
