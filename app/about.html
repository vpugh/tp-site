Driven by curiosity, a desire to learn and the need to problem-solve.

My name is Victoria Pugh, please call me Tori. I'm not good with titles so Front-End Designer/Developer sounds the best to me. I started as a freelancer and temp, working with companies such as: Lenox Inc, Saint Joseph's Univeristy, and the United Nations. My most recent position is at Dental Learning Systems, LLC.

My career began in print. I was taught that design is more then “making it pretty”, it is the culmination of creating and executing a solution; understanding your audience and their needs; and solving problems using critical thinking skills. This combination of design-thinking and execution is what I find most enjoyable and rewarding. Then I learned to code and took the skills I learned from my print and combined it with my inclination for digital design and coding.

I have a passion for learning. I can learn new techniques or programs at an amazing speed. I enjoy trying to find more efficient ways to create anything, and looking at new technologies. I am always expanding my knowledge, learning online, taking classes or reading articles.
