Nice to Meet You, I'm Tori Pugh

I have a passion for learning, design-thinking and problem-solving. These are skills drawn upon and developed from my beginning in print to my current digital realm. Using my "design is more than making it pretty" mindset and combining it with the forever changing nature of digital design and development I've found is an enjoyable and rewarding area.

I learn at an amazing speed, whether it is new techniques or programs. Thus, I enjoy trying to find more efficient ways to create anything, and looking at new technologies. Always seeking to expand my knowledge from learning online, taking classes or reading articles.

I like to share and interact on Twitter; tinker with code and learning new things on Codepen; and get inspiration and share my projects on Dribbble. In my sparetime, I try to work on my side projects or getting some time in on my PS4.
